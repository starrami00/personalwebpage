About Me

I'm Stephanie Ramirez (She/Her), and my fascination
with video games and programming has been a
guiding force in my life since childhood.
It all began when I delved into the captivating
world of robotics during the 5th grade, sparking my
passion for programming and setting me on a
path of exploration and discovery.

Throughout my high school years, I honed my skills
and showcased my abilities  by competing in
international robotics competitions.
These experiences not only solidified my
technical proficiency but also fueled
my ambition to channel my passion into something impactful.


I've continued my education by pursuing a degree in Computer Science: Game Design at the University of California Santa Cruz.
For me, the allure of video games lies in their potential
to evoke positive emotions and create meaningful connections.
Recognizing this, I made the decision to pursue a career in
video game development, merging my love for
programming with my desire to make a difference.

With a blend of technical expertise and creative vision,
I'm dedicated to crafting immersive gaming experiences that
leave a lasting impression on players.
My goal is to harness the power of technology to enrich lives
and inspire change, one game at a time.
